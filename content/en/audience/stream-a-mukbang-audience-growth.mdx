To effectively manage your **stream a mukbang audience growth**, you must understand the four pillars of engagement: Views, Likes, Followers, and Comments. | Statistic | Generation Method | Primary Purpose | Growth Impact |

| **Views** | Taking bites during active streams | Measures current broadcast reach | High (Immediate) |

| **Followers** | Cumulative streaming success over time | Represents permanent channel size | **Critical (Long-term)** |
| **Comments** | High-frequency eating actions | Adds live feedback and activity | Low (Atmospheric) |

The synergy between these stats is what creates a "viral" effect. As your follower count increases, your baseline for views in subsequent streams also rises. This creates a compounding interest effect where late-game growth feels much more explosive than the early grind.
